Emergency Roof Tarping in Plano TX

When a roof is too compromised to repair immediately — due to ongoing storms, structural assessment requirements, or after-hours limitations — emergency tarping is the first line of defence. We use commercial-grade 10-mil polyethylene tarps secured with battens and weighted edges — not consumer hardware store tarps that blow off in the next gust.

Proper emergency tarping for a Plano home involves:

  • Clearing debris from the damaged area before tarping
  • Overlapping the tarp well beyond the damaged zone — typically 2–3 feet on all sides
  • Securing with 2x4 battens screwed through the tarp into the decking at ridge and edges
  • Ensuring water drains off rather than pools on the tarp
  • Photographing the covered area for insurance documentation before tarping over it

What To Do While Waiting for Emergency Repair

  • Place buckets or towels under active drips to limit interior damage
  • Move furniture, electronics, and valuables away from the affected area
  • Take photos and video of all water damage for your insurance claim — document everything before it dries
  • If there is structural collapse risk, evacuate the area and do not re-enter until inspected
  • Do not attempt to access the roof yourself — storm-damaged roofs are unstable and wet surfaces are extremely slippery
  • Call your insurance company to report the damage — document the call time for claim purposes
