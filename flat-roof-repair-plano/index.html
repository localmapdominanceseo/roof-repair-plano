Flat Roof Specialists in Plano TX

Flat roofs are common on commercial buildings, additions, and modern homes in Plano. They require specialised repair techniques different from standard pitched roofing. Our team is experienced with all major flat roof membrane systems.

Flat Roof Systems We Service

  • TPO (Thermoplastic Polyolefin) — most common modern flat roof material, excellent heat reflection
  • EPDM (Ethylene Propylene Diene Monomer) — rubber membrane, highly durable in North Texas heat
  • Modified Bitumen — torch-applied or peel-and-stick, common on older Plano commercial buildings
  • Built-Up Roofing (BUR) — multiple ply system, still found on many older structures
  • PVC Roofing — chemical resistant, used in commercial applications

Common Flat Roof Problems in Plano

  • Ponding water due to poor drainage or settled areas
  • Membrane splitting or cracking from thermal expansion
  • Flashing failure at parapet walls and penetrations
  • Blistering from trapped moisture or adhesive failure
  • Hail punctures through single-ply membranes
