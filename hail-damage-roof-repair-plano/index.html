Hail Damage Roof Repair in Plano TX

Texas leads the nation in major hail events — in 2025 alone, the state logged over 900 major hail events. Plano and Collin County sit directly in the path of the spring and fall storm tracks that bring golf ball and softball-sized hailstones capable of destroying a roof in minutes.

How Hail Damages Your Roof

  • Bruising — hail impacts crack the shingle's fiberglass mat beneath the surface, invisible from the ground but catastrophic over time
  • Granule loss — hail knocks the protective granule coating off shingles, accelerating UV degradation
  • Cracking — larger hailstones crack shingles outright, creating immediate leak points
  • Flashing damage — metal flashing around chimneys, vents, and valleys gets dented and compromised
  • Gutter damage — gutters show clear dent patterns that help document hail size for insurance

Free Hail Damage Inspection

After a major hail event in Plano, most homeowners don't realise their roof was damaged. The damage is often only visible on the roof surface itself — not from the ground or the attic. Our inspectors climb every roof and document every impact zone with photos, measurements, and density mapping.

This documentation is exactly what your insurance adjuster needs to approve a full replacement claim.

Impact-Resistant Shingles for Plano Homes

When we replace hail-damaged roofs in Plano, we recommend Class 4 impact-resistant shingles — the highest rating available. These shingles are specifically tested to withstand 2-inch hailstones and many Texas insurers offer 15–30% premium discounts for Class 4 installations.

Hail Damage FAQs

How do I know if my roof has hail damage?

The most reliable way is a professional inspection — hail bruising is often invisible from the ground. Check your gutters for granule accumulation after a storm as a first indicator.

Does insurance cover hail damage in Texas?

Yes — most Texas homeowner policies cover hail damage as a sudden weather event. You pay your deductible; insurance covers the rest. We help document and file the claim.
