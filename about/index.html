Who We Are

Roof Repair Plano Texas is a roofing company built specifically for homeowners in Plano TX and Collin County. Our team works across every Plano neighbourhood — from the custom homes in Willow Bend and Hunters Glen to the established subdivisions in Preston Meadow, Whiffletree, and Deerfield.

We are local contractors, not out-of-state storm chasers. We know the HOA requirements for Legacy West and Willow Bend, the local building codes, and the specific roofing materials that hold up to Collin County's brutal hail seasons.

Why We Started

Plano's housing stock is predominantly 1980s–2000s construction. That means a large portion of local roofs are approaching the end of their lifespan at the same time that Collin County's storm seasons are becoming more active and more destructive. We built this company to give Plano homeowners a trustworthy, local option — with honest assessments and no pressure tactics.

Our Commitment to Plano Homeowners

  • Honest inspections — we tell you what we find, not what generates the biggest job
  • Written estimates — you know the cost before we touch your roof
  • No deductible waivers — we follow Texas law and will never put you at legal risk
  • Clean worksites — magnetic nail sweep and full debris removal every job
  • Written warranties — documentation provided on completion
  • Local knowledge — we know Plano's codes, HOAs, and conditions
