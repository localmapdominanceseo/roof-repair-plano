Why Plano Roofs Need Special Attention

Plano's housing stock is predominantly 1980s–2000s construction. That means many roofs in Preston Meadow, Whiffletree, and Deerfield are approaching or past their 20–25 year lifespan — exactly when North Texas's increasingly severe hail seasons are hitting hardest. Plano recorded 21 major hail events in 2025 alone.

Our inspectors are trained to identify issues specific to this era of construction: deteriorating underlayment, original flashing that was never upgraded, and ventilation problems that accelerate shingle aging.

Roof Repair Cost in Plano TX

Most roof repairs in Plano range from $300 to $1,500 depending on the extent of damage. Minor shingle repairs and small leak fixes sit at the lower end. Larger hail damage repairs, flashing replacement, or multi-section repairs cost more — and may be fully covered by your homeowner's insurance.

We always provide a written estimate after inspection. You know the cost before we touch the roof.

Roof Repair FAQs

How long does roof repair take?

Most Plano roof repairs are completed in a single day. Larger jobs take 1–2 days. We always perform a final walkthrough before we leave.

Will my insurance cover the repair?

If the damage was caused by hail, wind, or a storm, most Texas homeowner policies will cover it. We help document the damage and coordinate with your adjuster.

Do I need a repair or full replacement?

If your roof is under 15 years old and the damage is localised, repair is almost always the right call. We give you an honest assessment — we won't push replacement if repair will do the job.
