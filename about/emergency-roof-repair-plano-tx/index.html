If permanent repair can't be completed immediately — due to ongoing storms or after dark — we deploy heavy-duty emergency tarps to protect your home until full repair is possible. We use commercial-grade tarps secured with battens, not consumer tarps that blow off in the next rain.

Emergency Roof Repair FAQs

How fast can you respond to an emergency in Plano?

We aim to be on-site within 1–2 hours for active emergencies in Plano. Response times may be longer after major storm events when demand is high.

Do you charge extra for emergency calls?

We provide a written estimate before any work begins. Emergency tarping is typically charged separately from permanent repair.

What should I do while waiting for emergency repair?

Place buckets under active drips, move furniture and valuables away from the affected area, and take photos of all water damage for your insurance claim. Do not attempt to access the roof yourself.
