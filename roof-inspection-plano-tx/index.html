What's Included in Your Free Roof Inspection

Our comprehensive roof inspection covers every component of your roofing system — not just the shingles. We use our findings to give you an honest assessment of your roof's condition and any repairs or maintenance needed.

  • Shingle condition — granule loss, cracking, curling, missing sections
  • Flashing inspection — chimney, vent, valley, and edge flashing
  • Ridge cap assessment — condition and proper installation
  • Gutter inspection — attachment, drainage, granule accumulation
  • Fascia and soffit — rot, damage, ventilation gaps
  • Attic ventilation check — proper airflow to prevent moisture and heat buildup
  • Interior check — ceiling stains, attic moisture, insulation condition
  • Structural assessment — sagging, decking condition where visible

When to Get a Roof Inspection in Plano

  • After any major hail or wind storm
  • When you notice ceiling stains or active leaks
  • Before buying or selling a home in Plano
  • When your roof is 15+ years old
  • Annually as preventive maintenance
  • After a tree falls on or near the roof

Inspection FAQs

How long does a roof inspection take?

Most inspections take 30–60 minutes depending on roof size and complexity. We provide a written report with photos same day.

Do I need to be home for the inspection?

For the exterior inspection, no. However, we recommend being present for the attic and interior check portion.
